Our journey

Founded with a clear mission to bridge the gap between bold ideas and sustainable success, Alinaura combines deep investment expertise with hands-on business administration. We don’t just fund startups — we partner with founders to build resilient, high-growth companies ready for the global stage.
Headquartered in Er Riyad, we operate at the intersection of capital, strategy, and execution, supported by our sister company Riyada Consulting.

Vision

Why founders and investors choose Alinaura

Hands-on administration

Beyond providing capital, we deliver full operational support across finance, HR, recruitment, sales acceleration, and day-to-day business management. Our dedicated team works side-by-side with founders to streamline processes, reduce operational risks, and accelerate growth from the earliest stages.

Strategic advisory

Powered by the deep expertise of our sister company Riyada Consulting, we offer high-impact strategic guidance including market entry strategies, business model optimization, fundraising preparation, and international expansion. We turn complex challenges into clear, executable advantages.

Founder-first partnership

We build long-term relationships based on trust, transparency, and shared success. Unlike traditional investors, we act as true partners — providing mentorship, critical introductions, and continuous support throughout the entire growth journey, not just during funding rounds.

Data-driven investment

Our rigorous, research-backed selection process combines deep sector analysis, market intelligence, and proprietary evaluation frameworks. We invest only in companies where we see exceptional potential for innovation and scalable returns, backed by thorough due diligence and ongoing performance tracking.

Global network

We open doors to an extensive international ecosystem of industry experts, corporate partners, potential clients, and follow-on investors. Our network spans key markets in Europe, MENA, and beyond, giving portfolio companies unmatched access to talent, technology, and new opportunities.

Sustainable growth focus

We help build companies designed for long-term success, not just short-term hype. By embedding strong governance, scalable operations, and responsible innovation practices from day one, we create resilient businesses that deliver lasting value to founders, employees, investors, and society.
