Our solutions overview

Investment & capital partnership

We provide tailored funding and structured capital solutions for early-to-growth stage startups. Our approach emphasizes strategic alignment and long-term value creation, with flexible investment structures designed to support sustainable scaling.

Business administration & operations

Full-service operational management including finance & accounting, HR & talent operations, legal & compliance, and daily business administration. We build efficient systems that reduce overhead and allow founders to focus on product and growth.

Strategic advisory & consulting

Powered by Riyada Consulting, we offer high-level strategic guidance — market expansion, business model optimization, go-to-market strategy, competitive analysis, and fundraising readiness. Our advisors bring deep industry experience across key sectors.

Scaling & growth acceleration

Sales strategy, customer acquisition frameworks, partnership development, and international expansion support. We help portfolio companies rapidly increase revenue and market presence through proven playbooks and executive support.

Talent & organizational development

Recruitment of key talent, team structuring, leadership coaching, and organizational design. We help build high-performance teams and strong company culture that can sustain rapid growth.

Governance, risk & compliance

Implementation of robust corporate governance, financial controls, risk management frameworks, and regulatory compliance systems — essential for attracting larger investors and preparing for future funding rounds or exits.
